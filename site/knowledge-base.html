Reporting Road Issues

How to report potholes, damaged sidewalks, broken street lights, and other road-related problems.

  • Include the exact street address or cross-streets
  • Estimate the size (e.g. 12-inch-wide pothole)
  • Note any immediate safety hazards
  • Attach a photo for faster processing
Report a Road Issue

Trash & Pickup Issues

What to do if your trash or recycling was missed, or to report illegal dumping.

  • Report missed pickup within 24 hours of collection day
  • For bulk items, schedule a special pickup in advance
  • Illegal dumping: include precise location and description
  • Recycling contamination may delay collection
Report Sanitation Issue

Noise Complaints

Guide to reporting excessive noise from music, construction, or animals.

  • Quiet hours are 10 PM – 7 AM daily
  • Construction noise permitted 7 AM – 8 PM weekdays
  • For ongoing issues, log time and frequency
  • Animal noise: contact animal control directly
File Noise Complaint

💧

Water & Utility Issues

Reporting water main breaks, sewer backups, street flooding, and utility outages.

  • For active water main breaks, call 311 directly
  • Sewer backups in your home: contact your plumber first
  • Street flooding: report with photos and location
  • Power outages: contact your electric utility
Report Utility Issue

🌳

Parks & Trees

How to report park maintenance, damaged playground equipment, graffiti, and fallen trees.

  • Dangerous fallen trees: call 311 for urgent response
  • Graffiti: include photo and exact location
  • Broken equipment: note specific item and park name
  • Tree trimming requests: allow 30–60 day window
Report Parks Issue

Contact & Escalation

When to call 311 directly vs. using this portal, and how to escalate unresolved issues.

  • Call 311 for urgent or time-sensitive issues
  • Portal requests are processed within 1–3 business days
  • To escalate, reply to your confirmation email
  • Unresolved after SLA: request supervisor review
📞 Call 311

Resolution times vary by category and priority. Typical targets are:

• Emergency (safety hazard): 24 hours
• High priority (e.g., large pothole on busy road): 3–5 business days
• Standard (routine maintenance): 10–15 business days
• Low priority (cosmetic issues): 30+ business days
